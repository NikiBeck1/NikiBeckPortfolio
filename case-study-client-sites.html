Soma Massage

Industry · Brand + web

Visit site ↗

Soma Massage is a wellness brand specializing in therapeutic massage services. The project involved creating a modern, responsive website that reflects the brand's identity and provides an intuitive booking experience for clients. The main goal was simplicity and ease of use, ensuring that visitors could quickly find information about services, therapists, and easily book appointments. I focused on a clean design with calming colors and smooth animations to enhance the user experience while maintaining the brand's aesthetic.
