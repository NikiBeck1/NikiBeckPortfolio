About

Hi. I'm Niki.

Senior UI/UX designer who builds. Most designers stop at the handoff — I cross that line, which means my designs ship closer to what I intended.

I've spent 8+ years designing digital products, with a particular focus on custom B2B applications and consumer-facing websites. My background in both design and front-end development gives me a perspective most designers don't have: I understand what's technically feasible, how components get built, and where designs break in the browser.

At Trinity Apparel, I lead all digital product design — from warehouse operations tools to client-facing apps — and work directly with engineering teams using React. Before that, at Hamburger Creative, I designed and built responsive websites solo for clients across industries.

I'm currently open to senior UX/UI and product design roles, especially in health, wellness, or SaaS. Based in Birmingham, AL — open to remote.
